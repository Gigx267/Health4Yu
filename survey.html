Welcome to the Health & Wellbeing Survey

This study helps improve healthcare services across the continent.

⏱️ Estimated Time: 40–45 minutes
📋 Total Questions: 139
🔒 Privacy: Anonymous and confidential

Your participation helps us understand health needs better and improve healthcare services across Africa.

🧍‍♂️ 1. Demographics

Basic information to understand health disparities.

🌍 2. Migration & Language

Language and background affect healthcare access.

🎓 3. Education & Employment

Work and schooling data related to health quality.

🍎 4. Nutrition & Dietary Habits

We ask about your eating habits – how often you consume certain foods, how regularly you eat, and whether you have access to healthy food.

🏃‍♂️ 5. Physical Health, Activity & Body Measures

This section covers your physical health, activity, weight, height, and general physical condition. This information helps assess health risks.

🧠 6. Mental Health & Emotions

This section looks at your emotional well-being – whether you've felt stressed, exhausted, sad, or content. Such feelings have a strong impact on quality of life.

🏥 7. Health Insurance & Chronic Conditions

We ask about your insurance coverage and whether you've experienced any problems related to chronic conditions. This information helps identify healthcare needs more clearly.

💊 8. Medication & Treatment Use

This section explores which medications or treatments you currently use and how regularly you take them.

🍺 9. Substance Use (Alcohol, Tobacco, Drugs)

This section addresses your use of alcohol, tobacco, and other substances. These factors can significantly affect health.

💉 10. Vaccination & Preventive Care

We ask whether you've participated in preventive screenings and received vaccinations. Prevention is a key part of maintaining good health.

👪 11. Family & Children

This section relates to your family and your access to social support.

📱 12. Digital Health & Information

We want to know how you use digital devices to get health information.

🏁 13. Health Perception & Final Questions

In the final section, we ask about your general well-being, satisfaction with the healthcare system, and your feedback on the survey itself. Your input helps us improve.
